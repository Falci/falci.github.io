Personal Profile

I believe that every new technology, we developers should make a bet. We should bet on whether or not we devote ourselves to this technology. It is early devote to technology can bring great results if this technology is adopted by the market. I've been doing these bets almost 10 years ago, when I decided to get a degree in that area. I can not say I hit all today. But I can guarantee that over time it becomes easier.
